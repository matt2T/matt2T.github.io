The Online Baptist

Resources for your walk with Christ.

The Online Baptist Website is a welcoming resource for people from all walks of life who seek to grow closer to Jesus Christ.

As John 3:16, one of the most cherished Bible verses, clearly states: Believe in Jesus, and you will receive eternal life; reject Him, and you face death.

It’s not about how good or bad you are, as we all fall short of His glory and the wages of sin is death. Instead, acknowledge your need for God’s gift—His Son’s sacrifice on the cross—and embrace the opportunity to be born again through the miracle of the Resurrected Christ, who, as God, has redeemed humanity from sin and death.
